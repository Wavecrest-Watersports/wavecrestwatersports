Our boats Accesories can enhance your boat

We have created our first console for you, this console stands 1.12m tall has a width of 0.88m and a depth of 0.55m. Created in white and with a weight of nearly 40kg this is a large console, there is a 60mm flange around the base for easy fixing with either screws/bolts or glassing down. We have created the console using 2x 600 grams per meter of gelcoat, a 300 grams per square meter layer of chopped strand mat fibre glass (CSM) and 4x 600 grams layers of csm, its contains over 25kg of resin and has been highly polished to reflect the work you put into your boat.

The inside of the console is left untreated so if you are going to use it for storage you will need to apply a flowcoat or topcoat finish.
