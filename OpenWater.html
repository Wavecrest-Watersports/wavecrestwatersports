PADI Open Water Course

Learn how to dive independently of your instructor

We are not teaching you to dive alone!! Far from it, what we are doing is teaching you to be able to go away and dive with your buddy.... As a PADI Open Water diver you will be able to go to a dive store anywhere in the world, show your dive card and hire kit, this could mean you can dive without professional divers watching you...

So what will you learn? You are going to be taught how to plan a dive, how to prepare your kit, how to get your buoyancy right so you don't stir up the bottom, how to put on and remove your kit in the water.

How long will this all take? You will do 5 sessions (normally run one after the other so a day) in confined water (either a swimming pool or a beach with pool like conditions), you will also do 5 theory sessions ( don't worry it not like being back at school) where you will learn all the important theory of diving and then you will take 4 Open Water dives, yes in the sea, this is where you will demonstrate to your instructors that you understand how to perform the skills they have taught you in the pool.
