Equipment Specialist

Not all about what you have its how it works as well

Have you got your own equipment and wondered how it works? This course teaches you about what happens with your kit when its serviced, we will show you how equipment is assembled and how each piece works to keep you safe.

You will learn how to care for and maintain your equipment, this IS NOT a service course, you will not be able to service your own equipment this is simply a course to teach you how it all works and how to care for it better.

A great course for those of a mechanical mind or just an interest into the equipment you will be using.
