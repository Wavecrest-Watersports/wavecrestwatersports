Deep Diver

Take it to 40!

The deep diver course is a must for anyone wanting to take a deeper dive, with a limit of 40 meters this course will teach you the necessary skills in planning and executing deep dives, you will learn gas management and deep dive planning, it is also the first step into becoming a technical diver.

There is more to deep diving than just jumping in the water. You need to plan your gas use, plan the safety stops and what happens if things don't go to plan, how do you help your buddy and what does nitrogen narcosis feel like.

2 Days of 2 dives a day will teach you what you need to do to be a PADI Deep Diver, starting off slowly you will over the course of the 2 days’ you will work your way deeper until you reach the 40m limit, you will plan your dives as NO DECO dives working out dive time and surface intervals to obtain your certification.

This course we teach normally in Chepstow (NDAC) so will come with extra cost of accommodation, entry and air

£450 per person based on 3 students to 1 instructor
