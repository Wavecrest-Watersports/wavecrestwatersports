Technical Sidemount

Have you got what it take?

The world of technical diving is a challenging one and if you think you have the mental and physical strength then we are here to teach you.

Technical side-mount diving is as it says, this course will teach you how to setup and dive with side-mount rig and two decompression tanks, you will learn how to configure your kit, how to stage and de-stage your deco tanks and how to adjust your buoyancy, you will also learn how to perform bubble checks and buddy checks whilst hovering in the blue, without reference and ready to perform your dive.

Like all technical course we teach this course as Chepstow so extra cost is involved including accommodation, entry and air.

Are your ready to join an elite group of divers? Are you ready for the next big challenge?

Then come along and let’s get started

The price for this course is £450 per person plus equipment hire based on 3 students to 1 instructor
