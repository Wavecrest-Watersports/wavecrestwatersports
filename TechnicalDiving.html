Technical Diving

Have you got what it take?

The world of technical diving is a challenging one and if you think you have the mental and physical strength then we are here to teach you.

Technical diving is the most regimented form of diving, lots of planning and preparation goes into a technical dive and it works very much like a military operation, from gas planning to mental preparation of potential problems, equipment setup and agreeing your objective, checking and rechecking both yours and your teams kit is vital for this type of diving!

What do you need to be a technical diver? Firstly, good discipline, a good sense of teamwork and loyalty and most importantly of all the want to dive deeper and longer than a recreational diver, you must consider that unlike recreational diving you can't just come back up, there are decompression schedules you must adhere to!
