Our Specialties

Deep Diver



Learn the techniques and procedures to undertake dives to 40 meters

Underwater Navigation



How did you find that reef? Now learn the techniques we use to better enjoy your dive

Underwater Imaging



Do you want memories you can share with you none diving buddies? Then this is the course for you

Wreck Diver



Learn how to plan and dive on a wreck, from looking and mapping to full penetration

Self-Reliant Diver



Do you like photography but don't like to have to keep looking for your buddy? Than this course gives you more freedom to dive alone

DSMB Diver



An important skill to enable you to dive safely from a boat, let the skipper know where you are on your ascent and where you are on a drift

Night Diver



There is so much more life at night, more than you could imagine, from the fish feeding to the lobster looking for food

Search and Recover Diver



I've lost my...... Be the hero that helps your friends find what they have lost, learn the different techniques used in the recovery of sunken objects

Coastal Diver



Written by our founder Mark, this course teaches you how to plan dives in coastal waters, from looking at tide tables to checking tidal flow charts

 AWARE Fish Identification Diver



What was that fish? Now you can learn how to identify and group fish into families, as well as looking at the colours you can now lean to identify what you see

Ocean Reef Full Face Mask Diver



Don't like getting your face wet? The Ocean Reef integrated dive mask is for you, learn how to care for and use one of these fantastic pieces of equipment

Side-mount Diver



When one tank is not enough.... Side-mount diving gives you so much more flexibility, better positioning in the water and freedom of movement

Drysuit Diver



Diving cold and wet isn't fun and that' what diving is all about. English divers like to dive all year round and without a dry suit it gets very cold in the winter

Equipment Specialist



So, you have bought your own kit, but do you know how it works? This course teaches you the basic in how to look after your equipment

Emergency Oxygen Provider



As well as being a rescue diver Oxygen can help to save a life, can help to alleviate pain and in extreme cases to prolong the life or a seriously ill patient

Boat Diver



Dived from a boat? No well this course teaches you what you need to do to be a better boat diver, from where to stow you kit to teaching you how to enter and exit the boat

Dive Against Debris Diver



Plastic waste is all around us and this course teaches you how to survey and remove that rubbish

Enriched Air (Nitrox)



Dive longer with Enriched air, feel better and more refreshed when you leave the water and enjoy great dives whilst other have to sit on the boat

Peak Performance Buoyancy



Take your diving to the next lever, become a Jedi of the water and think where you want to be instead of using your BCD
