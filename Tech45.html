Tech 45

Have you got what it take?

This is the second step into technical diving, you will learn to use a single deco gas up to 100% oxygen for advanced decompression, like the tech 40 you will be able to show you can perform bubble and buddy checks, how to launch a marker, how to do multi stage deco stops, plan dives, gas usage and surface air consumption, shut down and gas switching, problem solving, you will work to and plan every step of the dive and dive every step of the plan.

You must be nitrox and deep diver trained and have at least 100 logged dives (30 at 30 meters or more) have approval from a dive doctor and be a tech 40 diver.

If you think you have what it takes to become a technical diver then sign up for this course but be warned this is not an easy course! Sorry to say but if at any time during the course we feel you can't perform to the standard required the course will be cancelled.

Price for this course £500 per person based on 3 students to 1 instructor
