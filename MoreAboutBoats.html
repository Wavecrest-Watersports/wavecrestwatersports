Our boats are made to the highest standard

Our 6 meter dive boats will be made to the Recreational Craft Directive and will have been extensively tested by the Maritime and Coastguard Agency meaning you can be sure the boat will look after you and your divers.

Fitted with twin 150 hp outboards giving you piece of mind that should the worst happen and you have an engine issue the other engine will give you all the power you need to return to safety!

The tube made by Tilley Tubes are again built to the highest standard and we know you will have years of service from them, built with internal baffles meaning should you have a leak in any one cell you will not have a catastrophic tube collapse, again your safety is our highest priority.

Hand built in our workshop using our qualified staff to ensure you get the boat you want, we have a huge range of colours to choose from so don't just buy a white or grey boat you could be daring and have bright red.......
