Welcome to Wavecrest Watersport.

Wavecrest Watersports now combines our three companies into one useful and convenient place.
We have decided to combine our passion for teaching scuba diving (Wavecrest Scuba) and first aid (Wavecrest First Aid) with our new venture of boat building (Wavecrest Ribs). So we welcome you along to our new site.

Based in the stunning county of Cornwall with its fantastic scenery from the towering cliffs of Lands End or Lizard Point (The most Westerly point of the UK) to the wide open moors of Bodmin and the famous Jamaica Inn, the incredible St. Michaels Mount, the spectacular beaches, or even the surf of Newquay, this land of legends and mystery with its wonderful clear blue seas is a magical land with years of history to interest everyone, let us teach you to dive and maybe show you some hidden gems of nature you might even learn a little Cornish history as well.
