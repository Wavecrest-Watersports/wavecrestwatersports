Wavecrest Watersports

 Founded in 2004 as Wavecrest Scuba the company has developed and grown, we became Wavecrest Watersports LTD in 2009 and we continue to expand our horizons.

Wavecrest Watersports encompasses our three passions in life, teaching people to dive, teaching the lifesaving skills of first aid and building quality boats and boat associated parts.

When Mark started Wavecrest Scuba, after moving back from Spain in 2004, he never imagined that the business would take so many turns as it and he have developed. Now a PADI Master Instructor Mark has progressed from a recreational scuba diver through the PADI ranks learning and teaching lots of different and interesting courses. Originally from Cornwall and having been a coach driver for years he knows a good bit of local history, so come on an adventure with us and discover a Cornwall you may never have seen or heard about before.

Mark was one of the first PADI TecRec instructors in Cornwall and has taken himself and the company to new depths, as a Technical Trimix Instructor he is qualified to teach to 75m and beyond.

Beccie our Master Scuba Diver trainer has worked in some stunning locations after taking her Divemaster in Egypt and working there she can tell you some amazing sites and helps to plan our Liveaboard dive holidays, Beccie came to Wavecrest to become an instructor nearly 5 years ago and has since become a vital part of the company, as well as a technical diver and Mark's partner. Beccie is a Cornish girl and loves the county, a keen horticulturist she can help you identify the wonderful Cornish flora
