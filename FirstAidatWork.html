HSE Emergency First Aid at Work


The 1 Day Emergency First Aid at Work course replaces the old appointed person first aid, this course will teach you all aspects of First aid as well as the regulations that keep staff safe in the workplace.

Learn life saving techniques that the emergency services learn from chest compressions and CPR to bandaging and treating shock, spinal injury and serious bleeding, a fun but serious 9 hour course awaits you, as well as the achievement of being able to help an ill or injured person.

Price based on 12 students to 1 instructor £150 per person (discount for 5 or more)

We will come to your work place (expenses for travel and accommodation to be added)
