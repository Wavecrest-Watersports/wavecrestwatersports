Advanced Open Water Diver

Are you ready for the next challenge?

Becoming a PADI Advanced Open water isn't as hard as you think....

Are you over 15 and an Open Water diver? Then you can become an Advanced Open Water diver.

So, what do you learn? You choose really! Yes that's right, you have to do two compulsory dives which are Navigation and a Deep Dive (Deeper than 18 meters) and then three elective dives, these could be a photography dive where you get to play at taking photos, or maybe a peak performance buoyancy course were we teach you how to fine tune your buoyancy or even a search and recovery course, were we hide something small and give you different ways to find it........
