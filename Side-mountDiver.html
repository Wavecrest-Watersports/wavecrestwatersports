Sidemount Diver

Seperating the twins

From the cave divers that started sidemount diving on basic equipment so they could explore deeper and narrower caves to the tech divers who prefer easy gas shut downs, sidemount diving has come a long way.

In this course you will learn how to prepare, kit up and use sidemount, this is a wonderful system and will not only help in your trim can even help in your buoyancy, you will learn shut downs, how to switch regulators and how to balance your system, you will learn safety drills and long hose buddy sharing.

The photo behind you can see Mark using sidemount is a tech configuration and this course can be the start or your tech journey, Tech sidemount diver is a great next step as well as being a stepping stone for Tech 40. 

So enjoy and remember it takes two to sidemount.
