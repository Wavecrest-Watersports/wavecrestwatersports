Coastal Diver

Its not all about the beach!

If you have never dived around the coast then this course is a must for you, written by our founder Mark it will teach you what to look out for in the sea, it could be the tide or a possible rip current, it’s all important to know how to control the situation and what to do if you get into a situation, how to get help and what you ned to do to help yourself.

You will learn how to read a tide chart, how to look at and understand a coastal navigation chart so you can work out what the tide will be doing at any state of the tide, what equipment you need to carry.

You might think you can jump in but what happens if the tide goes out and you don't recognise the area or on the other had your exit you marked is now underwater as the tide has come in, all things that you need to plan for.

Come on you don't want to get tide up, or left high and dry.
