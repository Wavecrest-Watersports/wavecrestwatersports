Night Diver

This course can elighten your sole

When everyone else is packing up from the beach you arrive with your buddy and start gearing up....

There is so much life on a reef at night that your won't believe your eyes, from crab and lobster walking round looking for food to sleeping fish, cuttlefish dancing their romantic dances and the magic of the weed by torch light, you will love this one.

Learn how to plan a night dive and over the course of 4 nights you will experiance your world being changed forever, you will learn how to set up a dive site for ease of entry and exit as well as how to ensure all team members can be seen, what to do in a torch failure situation and how to signal at night.

So come on what are you waiting for? Let us show you the light
