Wavecrest Boat Builders

We will soon be building 6-meter rigid inflatable boats, please check in again soon to see how we are getting on.

We do however build consoles and bucket seats at present.

Our consoles (constructed from 6mm Fibre Reinforced Plastic) are double console with a 10mm thick 100kg per square meter Armacel core on sloped front and top surface for mounting instruments, helm and throttle controls
