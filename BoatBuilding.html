Wavecrest Boat Builders

After years of working with other boats we have finally decided to start our own company making the boats we feel best suit the diving industry, not only will our boats be a little wider than the normal dive boat being just over 2.5m wide but we will be making them in a range of colours.

Our boats will have a wide range of uses from dive boats to pleasure boats and possibly film industry useable as stable camera platforms

Not an overly large boat at 6 meters but our rigid inflatable boats will be crafted by us, like all our mouldings from our seat and boxes to our consoles and hulls we have taken the time to build the moulds from scratch to give you the best we can offer, we hope you will like what we do.


Our consoles (constructed from 4mm Fibre Reinforced Plastic) are double console with a 10mm thick 100kg per square meter Armacel core a patented material made from the recycling of used plastic bottles on the sloped front and top surface for mounting instruments, helm and throttle controls creating a firm fixing rather than just the fibre reinforced surface.

Our future plan is to be able to build larger hard boats for fishing and diving and we will be showing you how we get on through our social media and photo galleries here in our site.
