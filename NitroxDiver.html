Enriched Air (NITROX)

Stay down longer and enjoy the water

The PADI Enriched Air diver course teaches you the techniques required to dive on air with up to a 40% oxygen content, meaning you can have longer bottom times than most other divers, learn how to calculate partial pressures and work out which is the best blend for the dive you are about to do.

Enjoy the feeling and take more photos than other divers, enriched air doesn’t extend your depth but your bottom times are, as you will absorb less nitrogen than regular air divers you get the advantage of lengthier divers, as must for Red Sea diving or those that love to take photos.

Enriched Air is a first step into the world of Tech diving so you are already taking the steps into the elite world.

This is a dry course but a dive can be included
