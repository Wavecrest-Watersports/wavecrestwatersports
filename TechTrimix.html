Tech Trimix

Have you got what it take?

Of all the technical course this is the hardest and longest, over the course of 8 dives you will learn how to obtain and analyse trimix blends of different rations dependant on the depth of dive planned, you will learn to understand the extra planning that goes into a deep technical dive and what is required, as per the other tech course shutdowns and bubble check as well as buddy checks and safety drills will be performed over each and every dive.

You will also learn as in all the other tech courses how to launch a marker and what to do in the event of a marker failure, you must be mentally ready for this course.

You must be nitrox and deep diver trained and have at least 100 logged dives (at least 30 technical dives) have approval from a dive doctor and be tech 50 qualified.

If you think you have what it takes to become a technical trimix diver, then sign up for this course but be warned this is not an easy course! Sorry to say but if at any time during the course we feel you can't perform to the standard required the course will be cancelled.
