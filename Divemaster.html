PADI Divemaster

Want to give something back?

Becoming a PADI Divemaster is one of the most rewarding things you can do as a diver. Taking the first step on the professional ladder can lead you into a world of fun and enjoyment and we mean a world. PADI trains more divers than any other organisation and is known worldwide so learn with us and maybe your next job is out in the Maldives, Mexico, Thailand, The Red Sea....... The possibilities are endless its only your imagination that can stop you!

So what will you learn? You will be there from the start of a divers journey, helping out with an instructor to teach students from Discover Scuba to Rescue Diver, Specialties and even other Divemaster’s, you will learn how to fill tanks and prepare dive sites, how to ready dive kit for customers and how to guide dives, this is a new opportunity for you to start learning again and now you become a role model diver.

Your instructor will become a mentor rather than a teacher and you will quickly learn how they work to get the best out of you.

PADI Divemaster’s are the backbone of a dive school and owners and instructors count on you for help with students, kit, keeping the shop clean, maybe even something simple like putting the kettle on after a day of diving, if you are prepared for hard work and getting stuck in then become a PADI Pro, you never know you could change someone’s life.......

Take the challenge, become a PADI Pro and live life to the full...... What are you waiting for?

£900 per person based on 2 students to 1 instructor (includes all manuals and materials required) plus PADI Membership fees
