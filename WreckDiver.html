Wreck Diver

Are you a metal head?

Most divers love to see reefs and the amazing amount of life but there are those amongst us that love to see wrecks! The life on a wreck is fantastic, to then learn the history of that wreck can blow your mind, finding out what caused the wreck, if it was a war grave or just bad luck....

We normally teach this course in Chepstow so there will be additional cost of accommodation, air and entry but don't let that put you off.

Over the course of 2 days and 4 dives you will learn how to survey a wreck, how to look for entry and exit points and how to plan the penetration dive, dive 4 will see you inside and learning how to reel off and back in through the wreck.

Most wreck divers are tech divers as well so this course can lead you into some amazing wrecks that others can only dream of!!

Don't miss the ship, not booking this course could sink your plans!......

£450 per person based on 4 students to 1 instructor
