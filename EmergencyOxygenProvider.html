Emergency Oxygen Provider

Better than a breath of fresh air

Oxygen is the vital ingredient in life and without it we would be in a whole lot of trouble.

This course will teach you how to set up and check different oxygen provider equipment from simple free flow to demand valve and when to use each one.

You will learn how to administer this life giving element to a ill or injured person and how it will benefit a sick or injured diver.

You don't have to be a diver for this course, you could be a first aider looking for a little more in-depth knowledge of learning how to care.
