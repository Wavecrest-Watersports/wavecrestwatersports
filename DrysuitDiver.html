Drysuit Diver

Its not all about being Bond!

Have you seen the films where Mr Bond walks out of the sea after a dive and strips to his trade mark white tux? Well that folks is a drysuit.

Now it’s not only for Bond it’s a great asset if you want to dive in the UK all year.

Here at Wavecrest we have accounts with Cressi, Sea & Sea so we can get dry suit in for you, if you purchase a dry suit from us then we will give you the course free, YES FREE.

This course will teach you how to prepare and care for your drysuit, how to enter and exit the water with a drysuit and how the diving is different in a drysuit, a hover is not the same in a drysuit as a wetsuit and you also need to learn emergency procedures in case of over inflation or sticking inflator, air in your boots and what to do if you find you are starting a feet first ascent

If you have a dry suit and have never been taught how to use it then we can do that for you as well.

We sadly DON'T have dry suits for hire for this course!

The price based on 4 students to 1 instructor is £150
