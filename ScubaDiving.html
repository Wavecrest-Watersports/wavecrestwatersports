Why learn to dive with Wavecrest?

Well why not? We are passionate about the sea and we love to show you what makes us smile, from the seals at Newquay to the stunning underwater scenary of Porthpean

Our instructors take pride in their work and put your safety at the top of the list as well as fun and excitement.
Don't be worried our team have years of experience working with small groups and they know how to get the best out of your diving, from the simple little tricks to help with your buoyancy to the more challenging diving of technical diving, you will learn in a maximum of 6 students to 1 instructor and most of the time not even that many, we don't run co urses when they are full we try to run them when you want them, unlike others schools that have strict timetables if you need more time to learn we will work with you to try to make this happen, if you can't do 5 days in a week for you Open Water not problem, we will work with you.

We have always worked on the theory that people learn when they are ready, if you start a course with us and don't feel ready to complete we will put the course on hold for you, there will be no extra charge for you to come back at a later date to complete, the only time this does not apply would be a technical course where we have cancelled it due to you not being able to meet the high standard required.

Mark our founder is a PADI Master Instructor and Beccie his partner is a PADI Master Scuba Diver Trainer, with years of diving experience and having dived some of the most amazing places in the world, they can help you not only plan and enjoy your dives but give you some great advice on where to go for your next adventure.

Mark and Beccie have worked in some wonderful places and have contacts all over the world, so why not become a Divemaster with us and see where the adventure takes you, you could be diving with whale sharks in the Maldives or exploring the Cenotes of Mexico........ The future is up to you
