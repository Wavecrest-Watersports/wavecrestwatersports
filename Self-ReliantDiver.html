Self-Reliant Diver

In the words of Yoda 'look after yourself you must'

Self-Reliant diver is a fantastic course for photographers and videographers, as well as dive guides, you will learn the techniques needed to be a safe diver if your buddy is too far away....

Learn how to switch to a separate air source that you carry, learn how to deal with problems yourself when you’re not close to your buddy such as cramp or entanglement.

You will also learn how to plan a solo dive to ensure you have the air capacity for the dive, how to ensure you are safe and how to make sure the right people know when are where you intend to dive.

Being a self-reliant diver is a good start on the technical diving route so come on join the dark side.
