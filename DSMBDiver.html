DSMB Diver

Its all about what you have up top!

As a diver you should always carry a Delayed Surface Marker Buoy (DSMB) maybe you and your buddy are on a wreck dive or just enjoying a quiet dive and you hear a boat, time to deploy the marker so they know where you are, maybe you’re on a boat dive and need to let the skipper know you are starting your ascent, these are all important things we need to be able to do as divers but sadly we see lots that don't know the correct way to deploy them.

In this course you will learn how to deploy a marker, how to deal with a marker failure and how to tow a surface marker.

Technical divers always deploy markers when they start their ascents so this is another course that works well if you want to join the tech diving fraternity.

So come on marker this one up as a must do.
