AWARE Dive Against Debris

It't not all rubbish

We have all seen the TV and watched the show that tell us how much plastic waste is in our oceans, the Dive Against Debris course will not only help you to understand how you as divers can help to prevent and reduce this but also how to log into the AWARE website to make what you have removed count.

We as divers are ambasadors for the underwater world and our actions can influence those around us, if you have ever beach dived and brought out trash you watch the number of people that will look around them and start to pick up rubbish, we are the future of our oceans and you can help to keep them safe and clean for our future generations.
